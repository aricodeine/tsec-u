Tsec-Committees

CODESTORM

Codestorm is a community of enthusiasts working in favour to promte technology and platforms to showcase technical talent among the student.It also conducts various workshops , coding competitions and hackathons.

CODECELL

CodeCell works towards nurturing interest and developing coding skills. We conduct various workshops and seminars about various languages and algorithms and helping students to build their resumes expand their skill set.

E-CELL

E-Cell aims at providing a pathway to the students to awaken the entrepreneurs within them.It also strives to bridge the gap between students and industrt. It organizes various webinars and events to promote entrepreneurship among the students

CSI-TSEC

Computer Society of India(CSI) is a body of computer professionals.CSI-TSEC is the student branch of CSI in TSEC. It aims of enhancing technical skills of the students, organizes a wide range of workshops, conferences and technical events.

NSS-TSEC

NSS-TSEC is one of the various NSS units working throughout the country.It has been jointly started by NSS(Mumbai University) and Thadomal Shahani Engineering College(TSEC).Its purpose is to create general awareness and conducting surveys regarding the way of life, infrastructure etc.

IETE-TSEC

IETE-TSEC is student forum of the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers.It organises various exciting events like ELAN and OSCILLATION and gives a very big platform to explore and learn amazing things.

IIche-TSEC

Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers is well known for its annual technical symposium, ChEMERGENCE.Every year, students of chemical engineering participate in various technical competitions which test their knowledge of fundamental principles of chemical engineering.

ISTE-TSEC

Indian Society for Technical Education(ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System

Clubs

Rotract Club

Rotaract Club of Thadomal Shahani Engineering College believes in changing the world with honesty, respect and humanity.It provides a platform to exchange ideas with leaders in the community, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service.

Tie Club

The Inner Engineering Club aims promote the AKA of Mental Health: Increasing Awareness that Mental Health issues exist, improving Knowledge on what these issues are, and providing Access to professional help.

GDSC-TSEC

Google Developer Student Clubs are community groups for college and university students interested in Google Developer technologies. By joining DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community as well.

MAD-TSEC

Mobile App Developers Club provides a platform for students to create an environment in the college where a group of talented students would come together, collaborate and work on real-life applications using trending technologies, thereby helping the students boost their technical knowledge and application skills.
